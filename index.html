Prevention of UTI and RTI Among Women Using Innovative Solutions

We proposed a randomized control trial (RCT) study to investigate the impact of innovative reusable cloth pads (improved cloth pad made of flannel cloth and water proof barrier sheet) with appropriate laundering practices, on the incidence of reproductive tract infections and urinary tract infections (UTIs) in women. Based on the eight school and eight readymade garments factory pilot testing experiences, the uptake of the of Sultana reusable cloth pad was over 76% among both women and girls and it reduced absenteeism both in schools (by 8%) and in the RMGs (by 1%).
